Why Expat
Employee turnover is a major challenge for companies today, especially when the labor market is competitive and certain skills are in high demand such as expatriates. When expatriates leaves, not only is the productivity of that person lost, but the routine of many others is impacted. Finding replacements can take months of time and effort on the part of hiring managers and recruiting staff, who are then forced to take time away from the work they could be doing. When a replacement is finally found, it takes weeks to months for that new employee to be completely onboarded and working at full capacity.
